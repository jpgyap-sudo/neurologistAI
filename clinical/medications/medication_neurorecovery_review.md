# Medication Neurorecovery Review

## Flag medication classes that may suppress arousal or impair recovery
- benzodiazepines
- sedating antipsychotics / dopamine antagonists
- anticholinergics
- sedating antihistamines
- excessive antiepileptic sedation
- opioids
- excessive antihypertensive dosing causing low cerebral perfusion

## Dopaminergic/arousal-supportive agents to review with physician
- amantadine
- methylphenidate
- levodopa/bromocriptine in selected cases

Do not recommend medication changes directly. Generate physician questions.

## Output
- medication name
- indication
- dose/frequency
- arousal risk
- interaction concerns
- physician question
