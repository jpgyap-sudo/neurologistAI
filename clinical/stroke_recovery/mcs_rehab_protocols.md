# MCS Rehab Protocols

## Track objective MCS recovery markers
- CRS-R score
- eye opening duration
- visual fixation
- visual pursuit
- command following
- localization to sound/pain
- reproducible purposeful movement
- swallow latency
- sleep-wake cycle
- head control
- sitting tolerance

## Rehab principles
- frequent, structured stimulation
- upright tolerance progression
- head/trunk control training
- passive-to-active limb training
- sensory stimulation with safety boundaries
- caregiver repetition logs

## Avoid over-interpreting reflexes
Swallowing and startle may be reflexive. Track latency, consistency, context, and volitional features.
