# MCS / Severe Stroke Recovery Tracking

Track objective, repeatable behaviors rather than impressions.

## Consciousness/arousal
- CRS-R total and subscale scores, if available.
- Eye opening duration.
- Visual fixation and pursuit.
- Command following: reproducible, inconsistent, absent.
- Localization to pain versus reflex withdrawal.
- Functional communication: yes/no, gesture, eye code.

## Motor recovery
- MRC grade by limb and muscle group.
- Passive and active range of motion.
- Spasticity: Modified Ashworth Scale.
- Sitting tolerance.
- Head control duration.
- Tilt-table angle and duration.

## Swallow/respiratory
- Swallow latency under therapist supervision.
- Cough effectiveness.
- Secretions/suction frequency.
- Aspiration events/pneumonia markers.

## Therapy dose
- PT minutes/day.
- OT minutes/day.
- ST minutes/day.
- Repetition counts for key tasks.
- HR/BP response and fatigue.
