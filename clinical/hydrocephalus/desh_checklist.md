# DESH Checklist

DESH = disproportionately enlarged subarachnoid-space hydrocephalus.

Check:
- enlarged Sylvian fissures
- tight high-convexity sulci
- ventriculomegaly out of proportion to atrophy
- narrow callosal angle
- relatively symmetric communicating hydrocephalus pattern

Dad-specific caveat:
Asymmetric post-hemorrhagic ventricular distortion reduces the reliability of DESH-like interpretation.
