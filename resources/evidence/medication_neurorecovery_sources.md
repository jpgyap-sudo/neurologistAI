# Medication and Neurorecovery Review Scaffold

The Medication Review Agent flags physician-discussion items only. It must not instruct medication changes.

## Categories to review
- Benzodiazepines
- Sedating antipsychotics
- Dopamine antagonists
- Strong anticholinergics
- High sedating antihistamine burden
- High opioid burden
- Excessive antihypertensive effect causing hypotension/cerebral hypoperfusion concern

## Neuroactivation agents to discuss only with physicians
- Amantadine
- Methylphenidate
- Modafinil / armodafinil
- Dopaminergic strategies in selected cases
