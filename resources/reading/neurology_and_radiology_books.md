# Neurology / Radiology / Rehab Book Checklist

Use as a purchase/library checklist. Do not store copyrighted PDFs in the repo.

## Core neurology
- Bradley's Neurology in Clinical Practice
- Adams and Victor's Principles of Neurology
- Merritt's Neurology

## Stroke / vascular neurology
- Caplan's Stroke: A Clinical Approach
- Stroke: Pathophysiology, Diagnosis, and Management
- Fisher's Stroke

## Neuroradiology
- Osborn's Brain: Imaging, Pathology, and Anatomy
- Diagnostic Imaging: Brain
- Neuroradiology: The Requisites

## Disorders of consciousness / neurocritical care
- Plum and Posner's Diagnosis of Stupor and Coma
- Neurocritical Care
- Disorders of Consciousness references from neurology/PM&R literature

## Rehabilitation medicine
- Braddom's Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation
- DeLisa's Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation
- Stroke Recovery and Rehabilitation
