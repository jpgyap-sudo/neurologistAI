# Open Neuroimaging / Medical Imaging Datasets

Check each dataset license and access terms before use.

## Stroke-focused

### ISLES — Ischemic Stroke Lesion Segmentation
- Website: https://www.isles-challenge.org/
- Use: acute/subacute infarct segmentation, commonly DWI/ADC/FLAIR depending on year.
- Relevance: useful for lesion segmentation workflow testing, not a direct hemorrhagic basal ganglia ex-vacuo dataset.

### ATLAS — Anatomical Tracings of Lesions After Stroke
- Website: https://atlas.grand-challenge.org/
- Use: T1-weighted stroke MRI lesion segmentation with manually segmented masks.
- Relevance: useful for chronic lesion/encephalomalacia segmentation concepts.

## General DICOM / medical imaging

### TCIA — The Cancer Imaging Archive
- Website: https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/
- Use: de-identified DICOM imaging collections.
- Relevance: DICOM import, anonymization, viewer testing, and pipeline stress testing.

## Neurodegeneration / atrophy / ventricular enlargement

### ADNI — Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative
- Website: https://adni.loni.usc.edu/
- Use: longitudinal MRI/PET and biomarker data.
- Relevance: atrophy and ventricular enlargement concepts; disease population differs from post-stroke ex-vacuo.
